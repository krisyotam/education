> - **Declarative** — definitions, theorems, formulas (spaced repetition / Anki)
> - **Procedural** — problem sets, exercises, proofs to write (learning by doing)
> - **Theory** — lectures, textbooks, deep reading (building understanding)

## Real Analysis

### Declarative
- Completeness axiom, sup/inf, Archimedean property
- Sequence convergence, Cauchy criterion, Bolzano-Weierstrass
- Series convergence tests (comparison, ratio, root, integral, alternating series)
- Continuity (epsilon-delta), uniform continuity, extreme/intermediate value theorems
- Differentiability, mean value theorem, Taylor's theorem
- Riemann integral, fundamental theorem of calculus
- Sequences and series of functions, uniform convergence, Weierstrass M-test
